Summing up Five Two-Digit Numbers

In this task, participants had 5 minutes to answer as many questions as possible. In each question, they had to sum up 5 two-digit numbers.
In the example below the correct answer is 21+35+48+29+83=216.

Each correctly solved question earned a point.
