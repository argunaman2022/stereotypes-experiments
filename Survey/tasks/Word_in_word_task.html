Word in Word Task

Participants had 2 minutes to solve as many Word in Word Tasks. In each task, there was a long word and they needed to find as many words as possible by rearranging the letters of the word.

In the example below two possible answers are "live" and "persuasive".


Assume that on average people scored 10 points. Do you think, on average, men or women scored more points? How large, do you think, the score difference is?
