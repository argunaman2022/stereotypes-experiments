Word in Word Task

Participants had 2 minutes to solve as many Word in Word tasks possible. In each task, there was a long word and they needed to find as many words as possible by rearranging the letters of the word.

In the example below two possible answers are "live" and "persuasive".


Do you think, on average, men or women answered more questions correctly? If the average person answered 10 questions correctly, how large do you think the difference is?
