Word Order Task

In this task, participants had 4 minutes to solve as many questions as possible. In each question, they had to make a sentence out of 5 given words by putting them in the correct order.
In the example below the correct order is : "4-1-3-2-5" which translates to "The weather is fine today".

Each correct question earned a point.

How many more questions do you think men (women) answered correctly, compared to women (men)? [in percentage points]
