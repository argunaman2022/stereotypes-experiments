Word Order Task

Participants had 4 minutes to solve as many Word Order tasks as possible. In each task, they were given a sentence consisting of 5 words in a scrambled order. To score a point, they needed to report the correct order of these words.

In the example below the correct order is: "4-1-3-2-5" which translates to "The weather is fine today".


Do you think, on average, men or women answered more questions correctly? If the average person answered 10 questions correctly, how large do you think the difference is?
