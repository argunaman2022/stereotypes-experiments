Creative Thinking Task

Participants had 2.5 minutes to solve 8 Creative Thinking Task. Each task included a reference shape and 3 candidate shapes. Participants had to visualize the candidate shapes to find the one that is identical to the reference shape.

Below is an example Creative Thinking Task:


Do you think, on average, men or women answered more questions correctly? If the average person answered 10 questions correctly, how large do you think the difference is?
