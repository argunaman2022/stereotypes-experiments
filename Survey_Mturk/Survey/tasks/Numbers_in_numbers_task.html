Number in Numbers Task

In this task, participants had 2 minutes to solve as many questions as possible. In each question, there was a big number and they needed to find as many summations consisting of at most 3 numbers of which each can be a number of 2 or 3 digits.
In the example below, two possible solutions are 103 + 14 (=117) and 14 + 43 + 60 (=117).

The more solutions they found, the more points they earned.

How many more points do you think men (women) answered correctly, compared to women (men)? [in percentage points].
