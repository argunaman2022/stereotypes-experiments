Word Puzzle Task

In this task, participants had 2 minutes to answer as many questions as possible. In each question, they had to find as many words as possible in a box with rows and columns of letters.
For instance, in the example below two of possible answers are "TIGER" and "BEAR".

Each word found earned a point.

How many more points do you think men (women) earned, compared to women (men)? [in percentage points]
