Title of task

In this task participants had to solve as many questions as possible. In each question, there was a big word (e.g. Persuasively) and they needed to find as many words as possible by rearranging the letters in the word (e.g. live, persuasive.) Below is an example
The more words they found the more points they earned. How many more points do you think men (women) earned, compared to women (men)? [in percentage points].
