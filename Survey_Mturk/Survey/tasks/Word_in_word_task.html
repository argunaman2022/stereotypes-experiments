Word in Word Task

In this task, participants had 2 minutes to solve as many questions as possible. In each question, there was a big word and they needed to find as many words as possible by rearranging the letters in the word.
In the example below two possible answers are "live" and "persuasive".

The more words they found, the more points they earned.

How many more points do you think men (women) earned, compared to women (men)? [in percentage points]
