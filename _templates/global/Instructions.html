Instructions.

Your Task
In this HIT you will be shown 10 different tasks that have been used in previous studies. For each of these 10 tasks we ask you to make an educated guess on how the average performance of men compare to the average performance of women.

In each question you will first see a description of the task. When you are ready to answer press the "I am ready to answer" button. Then submit your answer into the box that will appear using the "Next" button.

Earnings
After the last question, you will see your total earnings.
First, you are paid 2 USD for completion of the HIT. Second, in addition to this payment, for each question you are paid based on the accuracy of your answer. If you answer all questions fully accurately you will earn 2 USD+9 USD=11 USD.
